Book

The book resource provides services for getting available bible books. You can navigate through other services from here. That means you can fetch all available books with the URL /books or a specific book by requesting /books/{bookId}, or even navigating through nested services like /books/{bookId}/chapters/{chapterId}/verses/{verseId}.
