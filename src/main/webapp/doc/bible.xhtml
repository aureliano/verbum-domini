Verbum Domini

Bible

The bible resource is the API's root. You can navigate through all services from here. That means you can fetch all available bibles with the URL /bibles or a specific bible by requesting /bibles/{bibleId}, or even navigating through nested services like /bibles/{bibleId}/books/{bookId}/chapters/{chapterId}/verses/{verseId}.
